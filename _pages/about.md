<p class="intro-text">
Shayan Majidy is a <a href="https://banting.fellowships-bourses.gc.ca/en/home-accueil.html">Banting Postdoctoral Fellow</a> at Harvard University, working with <a href="https://lukin.physics.harvard.edu/">Misha Lukin</a> and <a href="https://rqs.umd.edu/research/research-groups/gullans-research-group">Michael Gullans</a>. He earned his PhD in 2024 from the University of Waterloo under the supervision of <a href="https://laflamme.iqc.uwaterloo.ca/">Raymond Laflamme</a> and <a href="https://quantumsteampunk.umiacs.io/people/nicole-yunger-halpern/">Nicole Yunger Halpern</a>. He is the lead author of <a href="https://www.amazon.com/Building-Quantum-Computers-Practical-Introduction/dp/1009417010"><em>Building Quantum Computers</em></a>, published by Cambridge University Press. His research spans quantum error correction, quantum thermodynamics, and applications of quantum computing.
</p>
